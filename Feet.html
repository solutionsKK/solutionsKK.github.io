NURVV

NURVV Run is a high-tech insole designed for runners. It collects key performance metrics and sends them to your smartphone in real time.

  • Tracks elevation and heart rate
  • Strava integration for social tracking
  • Footstrike Trainer to improve running form
  • Daily Run Allowance for fitness goals
  • Manual run logging for added flexibility

Sharkbanz – Ocean Safety Wearable

Sharkbanz is a wearable shark deterrent that uses patented magnetic technology to reduce the risk of shark encounters. Worn on the wrist or ankle, it emits an electromagnetic field that disrupts a shark’s sensitive electroreceptors, discouraging them from approaching.

  • No batteries, no charging required
  • Scientifically tested and backed by marine research
  • Safe for humans, marine life, and the environment
  • Used by surfers, swimmers, divers and beachgoers

Designed for comfort and durability, Sharkbanz offers a simple, passive layer of protection for people who spend time in shark-prone waters. It's water-resistant, impact-resistant, and does not interfere with other devices.

Ocean Tip: Sharkbanz doesn’t attract sharks or harm them — it simply makes you less interesting to their senses.
