NURVV

Foot-based wearable tech specialist NURVV has unveiled the availability of NURVV Run. Product features include: elevation and heart rate tracking, Strava integration, Daily Run Allowance, Footstrike Trainer, Metric Profiles, and the ability to enter runs not recorded with NURVV.

FOCI Focus Enhancing

Worn on your waist, FOCI captures your cognitive states and uses a stream of colored orbs to represent each state so you can see your mind and understand yourself better. When you lose focus, Mind Orb switches color right away. This increased self-awareness enables you to stay on track and focused.
