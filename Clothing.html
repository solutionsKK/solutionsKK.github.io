Owlet

These smart socks were designed to give parents peace of mind, especially when monitoring newborns during sleep.

Safety First! Owlet monitors your baby's heart rate and oxygen levels in real time, sending alerts to parents if anything changes suddenly.

The Owlet smart socks track heart rate, oxygen levels, and sleep patterns. Alerts are sent to connected devices if irregularities are detected, helping parents respond quickly to potentially serious issues.
