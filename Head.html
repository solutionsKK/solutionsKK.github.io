Muse Smart Headband

(eu.choosemuse.com, 2023)

The Muse headband is a brain-sensing wearable that helps users meditate by providing real-time feedback on their brain activity, heart rate, breathing, and body movements.

Unlike traditional meditation apps, Muse offers a more immersive experience by tracking your mental state and guiding you toward calmness using soundscapes.

  • Tracks brainwaves and helps reduce stress
  • Offers real-time audio feedback during meditation
  • Connects wirelessly with smartphones via Bluetooth

This smart headband is especially useful for beginners who want to develop a regular meditation habit and track their progress over time.

Did you know? Muse uses EEG (electroencephalogram) technology – the same tech used in medical settings – to monitor your brain activity!

NextMind Neural Interface

(Blooloop, 2021)

NextMind, now part of Cognixion, is a pioneering brain-computer interface (BCI) worn on the back of the head. It translates neural activity from the visual cortex into digital commands in real time.

Unlike traditional input devices, NextMind allows users to control computers and headsets with thought-based intent, creating a truly hands-free experience.

  1. Captures electrical signals from the visual cortex
  2. Enables direct brain-to-computer interaction
  3. Used in gaming, accessibility, and mixed reality applications
