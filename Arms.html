DnaNudge Arm Band

(Brown,n.d.)

The DnaNudge Band is a health-focused wearable worn on the forearm. It scans food barcodes and gives real-time feedback based on your unique DNA profile, encouraging healthier eating habits tailored to your genetic makeup.

  • Personalised health advice using DNA analysis
  • Barcode scanner helps guide food choices
  • Used in NHS research and wellness programmes
  • Pairs with app for continuous tracking

Airos Compression Arm Sleeve

(AIROS Medical,2024)

The Airos Medical Compression Sleeve is a clinically approved wearable used to treat upper extremity lymphedema. Worn on the arm, it uses pneumatic compression therapy to stimulate lymphatic flow and reduce swelling.

  • Worn on one or both arms
  • Delivers controlled pressure to reduce fluid build-up
  • Connected to a smart pump for adjustable therapy sessions
  • Used in hospitals, clinics and home treatment plans

Designed for comfort and effectiveness, the sleeve helps patients manage chronic conditions and improve mobility, all from the comfort of home.

Did you know? Airos compression therapy is often covered by health insurance due to its proven clinical benefits.
