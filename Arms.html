The Cicret Bracelet

The Cicret Bracelet is a wristband that turns your arm into a tablet, allowing you to use swipe, tap and pinch gestures along your own appendage instead of on a built-in screen.

  • Works on both wrists
  • Gesture-based input (swipe, tap, pinch)
  • No physical screen needed
  • Future potential for wearable computing

Gesture Control Armband

Thalmic Labs found a way to translate natural hand and finger movements through a wearable armband into actionable information for a computer.

Did you know? The Myo™ Armband is used in presentations, healthcare, and even military control systems.

Its product is called the Myo™ Gesture Control Armband, an enabling wearable tech platform with application possibilities including presentation control, medical, military, and assisted computer usability for those with disabilities.
